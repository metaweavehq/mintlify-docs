## What is this?

The Maritime Emissions Dashboard turns vessel consumption logs into the regulatory artefacts every shipowner now has to file: a CII rating, an EU ETS allowance bill, a FuelEU GHG-intensity statement, an EU MRV voyage report, and an IMO DCS submission.

You upload your fleet's consumption data once. The platform computes everything, every regulation, every vessel, every voyage — and gives you the dashboards, drill-downs, and downloadable reports you need to operate and to file.

## What you can do with it

<Steps>
  <Step title="See the whole fleet at once">
    A single year-selector drives the entire app. Every page — fleet overview, ETS, FuelEU, vessel detail — refreshes to the year you pick.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drill from fleet to vessel to voyage to leg">
    Click a row in the fleet table → land on the vessel page → expand a voyage → see leg-by-leg ETS coverage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch trends, not just totals">
    Daily timeline charts for CII, GHG intensity, and EU ETS exposure. Brush-to-zoom and target lines built in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the reports your auditors need">
    One-click downloads for IMO DCS, CII Statement, EU MRV, EU ETS, and FuelEU — formatted for the right authority.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who it's for

The platform is built for shipping companies operating between 2 and 100+ vessels who need to comply with overlapping IMO and EU regulations without standing up a data-engineering team to do it.

Typical users:

- **Fleet managers** monitoring CII ratings and EU ETS exposure
- **Environmental & compliance officers** preparing annual filings
- **Commercial teams** modelling EUA costs into voyage P&L
- **IT / digital teams** integrating the platform into broader fleet systems
