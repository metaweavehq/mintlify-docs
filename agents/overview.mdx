# Agents

Agents are autonomous AI-powered workers that can handle complex, multi-step tasks across your organization.

## What are agents?

Agents combine large language models with tools and memory to perform tasks that go beyond simple question-answering. They can reason, plan, and take actions on your behalf.

## Key capabilities

- **Autonomous execution** — Agents break down complex tasks and execute them step by step
- **Tool integration** — Connect to your existing systems, APIs, and data sources
- **Memory and context** — Retain information across interactions for continuity
- **Multi-agent collaboration** — Multiple agents work together on complex workflows
