# Skills

Skills are modular, reusable capabilities that can be attached to agents to extend their functionality.

## What are skills?

Skills encapsulate domain-specific knowledge and workflows into portable units that any agent can use. They define prompts, tools, and logic for specific tasks.

## Key capabilities

- **Modular design** — Skills are self-contained and can be composed together
- **Reusable** — Attach the same skill to multiple agents
- **Customizable** — Configure skills with parameters to fit your use case
- **Shareable** — Publish and share skills across teams
