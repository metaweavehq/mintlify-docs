## What it does

The **Purchase Analyzer** reviews a vessel's procurement pipeline end-to-end: PO approval lead-time, supplier delivery performance, budget compliance per category, and safety-critical-item status. It produces a senior review with prioritised actions and escalates blockers a Technical Superintendent must own.

It pairs with the [Purchase Data Collector](/skills/purchase-data-collector): one fetches, one interprets.

## When to use it

- "PO delays?"
- "Supplier issues?"
- "Budget overrun?"
- "Purchase review"
- "Procurement status"

## What it looks at

- Open POs by status (requested, approved, in transit, delivered, closed)
- Approval lead-time per PO
- Delivery performance per supplier (on-time vs late)
- Budget vs actual per category (technical, stores, lube, victualling)
- Safety-critical items in transit or stuck
- Aged POs sitting at any stage

## How it works

### 1. Approval lead-time

Per PO, lead-time at each stage is computed:

$$
T_\text{stage} = t_\text{exit} - t_\text{enter}
$$

POs sitting beyond stage thresholds are flagged:

| Stage | Threshold |
|-------|-----------|
| Awaiting approval | 5 days |
| Approved-not-ordered | 3 days |
| In transit (no ETA) | 14 days |

### 2. Supplier scorecards

Per supplier the script tracks:

$$
\text{On-time \%} = \frac{N_\text{on-time}}{N_\text{total}} \times 100, \quad \bar{T}_\text{lead} = \frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N} T_i
$$

Plus quality issues per shipment and recent escalations. A scorecard below threshold flags vendor renegotiation.

### 3. Budget compliance

$$
\text{Variance \%} = \frac{\text{Actual} - \text{Budget}}{\text{Budget}} \times 100
$$

per category. Variance over 10% is flagged; over 25% triggers escalation.

### 4. Safety-critical pipeline

Items tagged safety-critical get a separate timeline view. Any delay is treated as critical.

## What the review contains

- Executive summary with procurement health verdict
- Aged-PO table with stage, age, blocker
- Supplier scorecards
- Budget variance per category
- Safety-critical pipeline status
- Recommendations prioritised by impact
- Escalation decision

## Escalation

Escalates to the Technical Superintendent when:

| Trigger | Severity |
|---------|----------|
| Safety-critical item delayed | CRITICAL |
| Budget overrun above 25% in any category | HIGH |
| Supplier on-time below 70% | HIGH |
| PO awaiting approval &gt;14 days | HIGH |
| Stuck delivery &gt;30 days with no update | CRITICAL |
