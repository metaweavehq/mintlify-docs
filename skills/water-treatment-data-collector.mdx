## What it does

The **Water Treatment Data Collector** captures the latest water chemistry readings from every vessel system that requires treatment — boiler, cooling, potable, FWG output — along with chemical ROB, and files a timestamped snapshot. It pairs with the [Water Treatment Analyzer](/skills/water-treatment-analyzer): one fetches, one interprets.

## When to use it

- "Boiler water data"
- "Water chemistry"
- "Cooling water check"
- "Chemical ROB"

## What it captures

For each treated system:

- Boiler: pH, conductivity, hardness, phosphate, chloride
- Cooling: pH, conductivity, nitrite, chloride, microbial
- Potable: chlorine, hardness, microbial, salinity
- FWG output: salinity, conductivity
- Sample date and equipment running hours where applicable

Plus chemical inventory and consumption rates.

## How it works

1. Looks up the vessel by IMO.
2. Pulls the latest chemistry readings from the configured source.
3. Writes a timestamped evidence record under the vessel's water-treatment case folder.
4. Updates the case so any reviewing agent can see new evidence is available.

## What it produces

- Structured chemistry data consumed by the analyzer
- Plain-English summary covering systems sampled, headline flags, chemical ROB
- Filing metadata (timestamp, source, sequence number)
