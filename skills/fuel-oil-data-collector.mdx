## What it does

The **Fuel Oil Data Collector** captures a vessel's current fuel posture — remaining onboard (ROB) by grade, daily consumption, and the latest lab quality results — and files a timestamped snapshot. It pairs with the [Fuel Oil Analyzer](/skills/fuel-oil-analyzer): one fetches, one interprets.

## When to use it

- "Fuel check"
- "Bunker check"
- "ROB update"
- "Bunker data for AQUILA"

## What it captures

- ROB by tank and grade (MGO, VLSFO, HSFO, etc.)
- Daily consumption (main engine, auxiliary engines, boilers)
- Latest lab analysis with viscosity, density, sulphur, cat fines, water, sediment
- Stem schedule and supplier
- Recent bunkerings with quantity, supplier, BDN

## How it works

1. Looks up the vessel by IMO.
2. Pulls ROB, consumption, and lab data from the configured source.
3. Writes a timestamped evidence record under the vessel's bunker reports case folder.
4. Updates the case so any reviewing agent can see new evidence is available.

## What it produces

- Structured fuel data consumed by the analyzer
- Plain-English summary covering ROB by grade, days of fuel, headline quality flags
- Filing metadata (timestamp, source, sequence number)
