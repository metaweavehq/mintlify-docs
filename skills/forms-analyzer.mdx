## What it does

The **Forms Analyzer** reviews a vessel's submission record across mandatory forms (drills, inspections, reports, checklists), evaluates submission rate and lateness patterns, identifies recurring compliance gaps, and produces a senior review.

It pairs with the [Forms Data Collector](/skills/forms-data-collector): one fetches, one interprets.

## When to use it

- "Forms OK?"
- "Any overdue submissions?"
- "Check form status"
- "Forms review on POSUN"
- "Compliance gaps"

## What it looks at

- Total mandatory forms vs submitted
- Forms overdue and how long
- Submission lead-time vs deadline
- Repeat-delay patterns (same form repeatedly late)
- Owner-rank distribution of late submissions
- Compliance score across periods

## How it works

### 1. Submission rate

For each period the script computes:

$$
\text{Submission rate \%} = \frac{\text{Submitted on time}}{\text{Mandatory total}} \times 100
$$

A submission rate below the threshold (typically 95%) is flagged.

### 2. Lateness distribution

Late forms are bucketed:

| Window | Bucket |
|--------|--------|
| 0–7 days late | Acceptable |
| 8–30 days late | Concerning |
| 31+ days late | Compliance gap |

### 3. Repeat-delay detection

The same form being late across three or more periods points to a systemic issue — workflow gap, training gap, or unclear ownership — rather than a one-off slip.

### 4. Ownership analysis

Late submissions clustered under one rank or one officer point to capacity or training issues that won't fix themselves.

## What the review contains

- Executive summary with submission-rate verdict
- Late and overdue table
- Repeat-delay clusters (likely systemic)
- Ownership analysis
- Trend across periods
- Recommendations (process, training, ownership)
- Escalation decision

## Escalation

Escalates to a Technical Superintendent when:

| Trigger | Severity |
|---------|----------|
| Submission rate below 90% | HIGH |
| Form overdue 31+ days | CRITICAL |
| Repeat delay across 3+ periods | HIGH |
| Safety-critical form overdue | CRITICAL |
