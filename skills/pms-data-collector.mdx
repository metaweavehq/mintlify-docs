## What it does

The **PMS Data Collector** captures the current state of a vessel's planned maintenance — every job's due date, last completion, status, criticality, and equipment — and files a timestamped snapshot. It pairs with the [PMS Analyzer](/skills/pms-analyzer): one fetches, one interprets.

## When to use it

- "Maintenance data"
- "PMS status"
- "Check overdue jobs"
- "PMS check"

## What it captures

- Every PMS job for the vessel: equipment, description, frequency, due date, last done, status
- Criticality (CRITICAL / MAJOR / ROUTINE)
- Running hours where applicable
- Recently completed jobs with completion date
- Stuck jobs with blocker (parts, port, vendor)

## How it works

1. Looks up the vessel by IMO.
2. Pulls the PMS job list from the configured source.
3. Writes a timestamped evidence record under the vessel's PMS case folder.
4. Updates the case so any reviewing agent can see new evidence is available.

## What it produces

- Structured PMS data consumed by the analyzer
- Plain-English summary covering total jobs, on-time count, overdue count, criticality split
- Filing metadata (timestamp, source, sequence number)

## Related skills

- **pms-analyzer** — senior skill that turns this snapshot into a maintenance review
- **defect-data-collector** — sister collector for defect tracking
- **status-rollup** — fleet-wide aggregation
