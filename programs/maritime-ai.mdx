## Overview

The Maritime AI Program is the Foundation's first industry vertical. We build AI systems that understand maritime operations — not generic tools adapted for shipping, but systems designed from day one with maritime domain expertise.

## Why maritime?

- **90% of global trade** moves by sea
- **High complexity** — regulatory, technical, commercial, and operational dimensions
- **Low AI adoption** — most companies still run on email and spreadsheets
- **Clear value** — predictive maintenance, compliance automation, operational efficiency

## Solutions

### Fleet management
AI agents managing daily vessel operations:
- Autonomous email processing and classification
- Case management with full lifecycle tracking
- Maintenance scheduling and dry-dock coordination
- Certificate renewal and regulatory compliance

### Emissions monitoring
Real-time tracking and regulatory compliance:
- CII rating calculation and projections
- EU MRV and IMO DCS automated reporting
- Fleet-wide emissions benchmarking
- Fuel consumption optimization insights

### Voyage analytics
Performance intelligence for fleet operators:
- Engine SFOC (Specific Fuel Oil Consumption) analysis
- Weather-normalized voyage comparisons
- Speed-consumption optimization curves
- Historical performance trend tracking

### Procurement
Fleet-wide spend management:
- Requisition-to-delivery tracking
- Vendor quote comparison and selection
- Cycle time analysis with bottleneck detection
- Spend analytics by vessel, category, and vendor
