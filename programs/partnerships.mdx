## Partnership model

The Foundation builds open source AI platforms. Partners deploy, customize, and provide domain expertise.

## Partner types

### Implementation partners
Organizations that deploy Lifeverse AI for their clients.

- Access to the full platform and documentation
- Direct communication with Foundation engineers
- Contribute deployment learnings back to the project
- Revenue from implementation and consulting services

**Current**: [MetaWeave Consulting](https://metaweave.in) — maritime shipping operations

### Industry partners
Companies that use the platform and provide domain expertise.

- Deploy Lifeverse AI for their operations
- Provide feedback on workflows and domain requirements
- Share anonymized operational patterns for organizational learning
- Get priority support and early access to new features

### Research partners
Academic institutions and research labs.

- Collaborate on multi-agent systems research
- Access to anonymized deployment data
- Joint publications and conference presentations
- Student placement opportunities

## Become a partner

We're looking for partners in:

- **Maritime shipping** — fleet operators, ship managers, maritime service providers
- **Logistics & supply chain** — freight forwarders, warehouse operators, 3PL providers
- **Energy** — utility operators, renewable energy companies
- **AI research** — universities and labs working on multi-agent systems
