## Who we are

The Applied AI Foundation is a non-profit initiative dedicated to building open source AI infrastructure for industries that power civilization. We believe AI should be a shared resource, not a competitive moat.

Founded in partnership with [MetaWeave Consulting](https://metaweave.in), we combine deep industry expertise with cutting-edge AI research to create systems that any company can deploy and customize.

## What we believe

> "AI for everyone in the world, every company in the world."

- **AI is infrastructure**, not a product. Like electricity and the internet, intelligent systems should be accessible to all.
- **Domain expertise matters**. Generic AI tools fail in specialized industries. We build with operators, not for them.
- **Open source wins**. When the whole industry contributes, the whole industry benefits. Proprietary AI creates fragile dependencies.

## What we do

<Steps>
  <Step title="Research">
    We research multi-agent architectures, persistent memory systems, and autonomous operations for industrial applications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build">
    We build open source platforms — starting with Lifeverse AI — that any organization can deploy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Partner">
    We work with industry partners like MetaWeave to validate, deploy, and iterate on real-world implementations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share">
    Everything we learn goes back to the community. Research papers, code, operational patterns — all open.
  </Step>
</Steps>
