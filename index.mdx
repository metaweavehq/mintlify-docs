## Our philosophy

Traditional enterprise AI locks intelligence behind proprietary walls. We believe the industries that power civilization — shipping, logistics, energy — deserve AI that is:

- **Open** — MIT licensed, no vendor lock-in
- **Persistent** — agents that remember and learn across sessions
- **Collaborative** — multiple agents working as an organization
- **Industry-native** — built with domain expertise, not generic templates
